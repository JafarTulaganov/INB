About company

Since the foundation in 1995, our company has always developed according to the concept of "user-oriented" sewing equipment.

About company

The IMB automatic sewing machine products developed and put into production have been highly appreciated by users and industry experts. Today we are a prominent market player in the field of automatic aggregates and the modernization of the garment industry. We plan together with clients according to goals and budget in order to find the best investment balance. We work with total designs, existing designs, flowsheets, time tables, layouts, energy bills and everything else needed for a successful plant. With the help of specially developed software, we can quickly calculate your plant's situation, efficiency margin, and provide solutions for improvement, respectively, prioritizing the situation and step by step improvements. IMB currently has 220 employees, excluding 30 R&D employees. And it has premises with a total area of ​​30,000 square meters! "Our highly educated and experienced staff strives to provide the best quality in the shortest possible time to our valued partners and customers. This is our vision and the reason why IMB has been able to grow so quickly over the years!" Our after-sales service is ready to help 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, for any question. IMB is located in the beautiful city of Changzhou, Jiangsu Province, which is easily accessible. reachable from Shanghai by car (1.5 hours) or high speed train (40 minutes) "With the world's first interactive online directory, we give you a better understanding of our company and products. You will find our products by category, a brief description of the main functions , as well as movies for each product.Check back regularly for this catalog, it will always be updated with new products and services!Please feel free to contact us and be part of the IMB family!
